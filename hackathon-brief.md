## Tracks

### Track 1 — Personal AI Agents to Organize Your Life ($1,000 CAD)
AI agents that act like personal assistants, operators, coordinators, or companions across work, home, travel, health, family. Beyond chatbots: take action, manage complexity, remember context, help people reclaim time and energy.

### Track 2 — Tangerine: AI Agents to Organize Your Financial Life ($1,000 CAD)
AI agents that help individuals and families understand, organize, and optimize finances. Proactive: identify risks, opportunities, wasteful spending. Examples: budgeting copilots, subscription/bill management, savings goal planners, debt payoff strategies, financial literacy, tax prep, document organization.

## Judging Criteria (30 pts total)

1. **Real-world problem (10 pts)** — meaningful pain points in productivity, home, travel, family, health, scheduling, communication, finances.
2. **Creativity / delight (10 pts)** — bold ideas, memorable interactions, agent experiences that feel surprisingly helpful, human, or magical.
3. **Thoughtful use of AI agents (10 pts)** — beyond chat: action-taking, context retention, task coordination, decision-making, workflows that wouldn't otherwise exist.

## Sponsor Strategy (Track 2)

Judges weight by the 3 published criteria (real-world problem, creativity/delight, agent thoughtfulness). Sponsor tech is not formally scored, but two factors matter:

1. Tangerine owns Track 2 and provides **2 of 6 judges** (CTO + Head of AI). Frame the pitch in their language: family financial confidence, stress reduction, proactive coaching.
2. Backboard's co-founder is judging. Their stack (17k LLMs + memory + RAG + web search behind one API, coupon `TECHTO26`) maps directly to the receipts-to-queryable-DB pipeline.
3. ElevenLabs adds the "delight" criterion cheaply via a voice front door ("How much did we spend in Lisbon on food?").

Codalio (no-code) and Rootly (SRE) are weak fits — don't force them in.
